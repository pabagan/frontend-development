About

We are Carofile.
And we’re transforming car ownership and maintenance.

At Carofile, we bring together a talented team of engineers and developers with a passion for changing the way cars are owned, managed, and maintained.

The simplest way to manage your car.
Supported by the most sophisticated technology.

Having worked across car-based technology for some of the world’s biggest motoring brands, we saw the issues with owning a car.

The way useful information on repairs was indecipherable from just a few dashboard lights. The way people felt when dealing with garages and mechanics. The sheer number of things to keep up with, with your MOT, insurance, tax and services all completely separate from one another.

We knew there was a better way: to bring every aspect of a car’s health together in one place. Carofile was born.
